Instructions

 There are a few checkboxes in which you can set up your personalized training. Depending upon the selected ones, this will arrange the symbols displayed.

 You will also find a Japanese character, a button, and a writing space. Once you see and recognize the symbol, type the lecture in and press "pass"; Automatically, the app will tell you whether you wrote it correctly or wrong. Then a new character will appear on the screen. You can repeat the process the times you want.
